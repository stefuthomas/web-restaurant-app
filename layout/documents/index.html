About us

MunchFinder is your one-stop solution for finding student-friendly restaurants and viewing their daily and weekly menus. We understand the needs of students and aim to make meal planning easier and more affordable. Browse through a wide variety of restaurants, compare menus, and find the perfect meal that suits your taste and budget.

On our website you can:

  • Search for restaurants by name or location
  • View daily and weekly menus
  • View restaurant locations on a map
  • Sign up to save your restaurant preferances
